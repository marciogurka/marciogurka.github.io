Training

What a brain can do if the all the rest of the body is not healthy as it should be to working at his full potential??? That's why I love to train my body as much I love to train my brain.

I always enjoyed playing football and volleyball with some friends a few times per week, but since I completed my graduation I didn't have that much free time to keep my body strong.

But then in 2017 I discovered Crossfit, and it was love since the first sight! It's something that I can't live without now. I love all the environment, the people and the workouts (not all of them, but that's normal). I'm doing Crossfit at Crossfit 1530 and I hope to keep it like that forever.

Like I said, I think that have a healthy body it's important to keep the brain and mind working at his full potential, and having some fun with the people at the box while we are doing the workouts are the reasons that I love Crossfit.

That kind of experience is something that opens your mind and makes you think in others points of view of different situations, and I believe that the sharing of culture aggregates a lot to you as a human being.
